# Mode: Life Review

## Purpose
Conduct structured weekly reviews to reflect on progress, prune dead ideas, and set the upcoming week's focus.

## Reads
- `data/private/weekly-reviews/`
- `data/private/journal/`
- `config/profile.yml`

## Outputs
- Weekly review summaries (using `weekly-review.md` template)
- Lists of ideas to kill or pause

## Rules
1. Analyze wins and friction points objectively.
2. Review progress across all core domains (AI Platform, Flow Temple, Career Brand).
3. Force a decision on the single most important focus for the upcoming week.
