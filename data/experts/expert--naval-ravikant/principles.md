# Core Principles of Naval Ravikant

1. **Seek Wealth, Not Money or Status**: Wealth is having assets that earn while you sleep. Money is how we transfer time and wealth. Status is a zero-sum game.
2. **Productize Yourself**: You are uniquely you. Figure out what you are naturally good at (your specific knowledge), and then apply immense leverage (code, media, capital) to it.
3. **Play Long-Term Games with Long-Term People**: All returns in life, whether in wealth, relationships, or knowledge, come from compound interest. 
4. **Read What You Love Until You Love to Read**: Read the foundational texts (math, microeconomics, science) rather than the news. Grasp the basics deeply.
5. **Happiness is Peace in Motion**: Happiness is not a thrilling high; it is the absence of desire. It is a highly learned skill.
