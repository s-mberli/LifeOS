# The Wealth and Leverage Playbook

## Acquiring Specific Knowledge
- Follow your genuine intellectual curiosity rather than whatever is currently making money.
- If it entertains you but bores others, that is a signal of specific knowledge.
- Specific knowledge cannot be taught, but it can be learned. Often it is on the bleeding edge of technology or art.

## Applying Leverage
- **Escape the Hourly Trap**: Never rent out your time. If you must, charge a radically high hourly rate to force yourself to delegate or automate.
- **Build Permissionless Leverage**: Learn to code, or learn to write/record media. These assets work for you 24/7 without needing anyone's permission.
- **Take Accountability**: Take risks under your own name. Society will reward you with responsibility, equity, and leverage when you bear the downside.

## Decision Making
- **The "Hell Yes" Rule**: If you have to choose between two options and you can't decide, the answer is no. If it isn't a "Hell Yes," it's a no.
- **The Hard Path**: If there are two paths and they are equally difficult, choose the one that is more painful in the short term. The brain tends to avoid short-term pain, even if it leads to long-term gain.
