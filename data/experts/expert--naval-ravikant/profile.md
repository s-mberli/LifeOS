# Naval Ravikant

## Core Philosophy
Wealth is not about having a lot of money; it's about having assets that earn while you sleep. Happiness is a highly learned skill that you can cultivate by deeply understanding your desires and reducing them. 

## Key Frameworks
- **Specific Knowledge**: Knowledge that you cannot be trained for. If society can train you, it can train someone else and replace you.
- **Leverage**: The use of capital, labor, code, or media to multiply your effort. Code and media are permissionless leverage.
- **Play as Work**: Find work that feels like play to you, but looks like work to others.
