# Dalai Lama

## Core Philosophy
The ultimate source of happiness is within ourselves. Compassion and warm-heartedness are the foundations of a meaningful life and a peaceful world.

## Key Frameworks
- Universal Responsibility: Recognizing that we are all interconnected.
- Secular Ethics: Moral principles based on human nature rather than religious doctrine.
