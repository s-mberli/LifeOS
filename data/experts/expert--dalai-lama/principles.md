# Core Principles of the Dalai Lama

1. **Warm-Heartedness Over Intelligence**: Intellect without warm-heartedness is dangerous. A brilliant mind must be guided by a compassionate heart to be truly useful to humanity.
2. **Universal Responsibility**: You are part of an interconnected web of life. Your actions ripple out and affect others. Act with the awareness that everyone else simply wants to be happy, just like you.
3. **Inner Peace Precedes Outer Peace**: World peace cannot be achieved through treaties or force; it begins within the minds of individuals. If you have inner peace, you project peace into the world.
4. **Destructive vs. Constructive Emotions**: Learn to differentiate between emotions that destroy peace (anger, jealousy, greed) and those that construct it (compassion, patience, forgiveness).
5. **Secular Ethics**: Ground your morality not in religious dogma, but in the biological reality that we are social animals who depend on one another.
